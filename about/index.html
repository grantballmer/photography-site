I am an amateur photographer currently living and working in South Korea. For the past 5 years I've been employed as an English Teacher here. During that time, I've taken advantage of any free weekend or holiday to explore all throughout East and Southeast Asia, snapping photographs of my experiences along the way.


My current setup is a Nikon D3300 with a Tokina 11-16 f/2.8, Sigma 17-50 f/2.8, and a Nikon 70-300 AF-P VR. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me at my email: grant.a.ballmer@gmail.com
